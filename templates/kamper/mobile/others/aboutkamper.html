How it all began..

KAMPER KOREA was founded in 2016 with a single mission in mind:
TO HELP INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS and EXPATS IN KOREA

Living abroad is difficult.
Our team want to help and give support to those who are having troubles in Korea.
We provide the essentials to settle in Korea , as well as other
necessities like sim-cards and housing.
We are also working on travelling and meet ups
so you can enjoy Korea just like the locals.


Our final goal is to be
"The ultimate solution for your stays in Korea"
